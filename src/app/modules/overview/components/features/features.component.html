Features

Gas-Free Transactions

Say goodbye to gas fees! With Tonkeeper Battery, you can perform transactions without the need for TONs. The battery automatically covers all blockchain fees, making your crypto experience smoother and more cost-effective.

Multi-Token Support

Tonkeeper Battery supports a wide range of tokens, including stablecoins, NFTs, and other cryptocurrencies. This flexibility allows you to manage and transact with various digital assets effortlessly, all in one place.

Simple Top-Up Process

Charging your Tonkeeper Battery is a breeze. You can easily top up your battery using in-app payments on iOS or by sending TONs or stablecoins to the special address battery.ton. This straightforward process ensures that you are always ready for your next transaction.

Real-Time Charge Monitoring

Keep track of your battery status in real-time. The Tonkeeper app provides a clear and detailed view of your battery charges and usage, so you always know how many charges are available and can plan your transactions accordingly.
