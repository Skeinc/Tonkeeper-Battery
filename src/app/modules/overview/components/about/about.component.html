What is Tonkeeper Battery?

Welcome to Tonkeeper Battery, your gateway to seamless and gas-free cryptocurrency transactions. Our mission is to simplify the crypto experience for everyone by eliminating the need for complex and costly gas fees. With Tonkeeper Battery, you can now easily manage your digital assets without worrying about blockchain transaction fees.

Tonkeeper Battery is currently available in open beta on all mobile platforms, offering a revolutionary way for new users to start using stablecoins, NFTs, and other tokens immediately. No more hassle of registering on exchanges or navigating through complicated procedures just to pay for gas.

How it works?

Tonkeeper Battery automatically covers blockchain fees, allowing you to pay with tokens (stablecoins, NFTs, etc.) without purchasing TONs for gas. Charge your battery using in-app payments on iOS or with TONs or stablecoins. Simply send a few dollars in stablecoins or TONs to the special address battery.ton, and your wallet will be charged.

Each charge covers the fee for a simple TON transfer. More complex transactions may require multiple charges. The battery is measured in charges that cover those network fees. For example, token swaps require ~50 charges, token transfers ~10 charges, and NFT transfers ~4 charges.
